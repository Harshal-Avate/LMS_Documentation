# 1. Login

The Login page is the entry point to the SimpleSphere Student Portal.
Students use their credentials to securely access the system.

## 1.1 How to Access

- Open your web browser and navigate to the SimpleSphere LMS URL
  provided by your institution.

- You will be directed to the Login page automatically.

## 1.2 Login Steps

- Step 1 -- Enter your Username in the \'Username\' field.

- Step 2 -- Enter your Password in the \'Password\' field. Click the eye
  icon to show or hide the password.

- Step 3 -- (Optional) Check \'Stay logged in\' to remain logged in
  across browser sessions.

- Step 4 -- Click the Sign In button.

- Step 5 -- Alternatively, click Sign in with Google to log in using
  your Google account.

> **📌 Note:** If you have forgotten your password, click \'Forgot
> Password?\' to receive a reset link.

# 2. Student Dashboard (Overview)

The Dashboard is your home page. It gives a quick summary of your
learning activity, upcoming sessions, and pending tasks.

## 2.1 How to Access

- After login you land here automatically.

- Click \'Overview\' in the left sidebar under MY DASHBOARD.

## 2.2 Dashboard Sections

### Live Class Banner

If a mentor has started a live session, a purple animated banner appears
at the top showing the session title and mentor name. Click Join Now to
open the meeting link in a new tab.

### Survey Notification

If a feedback survey is pending for one of your courses, a banner
appears prompting you to take it. Click Start Survey and answer the
questions, then click Submit Feedback.

### Greeting & Activity Overview

A personalized greeting with your first name is shown along with
\'Activity Overview\'.

### Stat Cards

Four cards display at a glance:

- Enrolled Courses -- number of courses you are enrolled in.

- Attendance -- your overall attendance percentage.

- Pending Tasks -- number of assessments not yet submitted.

- Certificates -- total certificates earned.

Clicking any stat card navigates to the related section.

### Continue Learning

Lists your enrolled courses. Click a course row to open it and resume
learning.

### Upcoming Sessions

Shows scheduled live sessions with time and date. Click the link icon to
open the meeting.

### Pending Assessments

Shows assessments assigned to you that have not been submitted. Overdue
items are highlighted in red. Click View All to go to the Assessments
page.

### Quick Actions

Buttons to quickly navigate to: Split-View Workspace, AI Study Mentor,
My Analytics, Achievements.

### Interactive Lab Promo

A green card that opens the Split View workspace where you can watch a
lecture and write code simultaneously.

### AI Study Mentor Promo

A purple card that opens the AI chat assistant for study help.

# 4. My Courses

The Courses page is where you access all enrolled courses and explore
new ones available on the platform.

## 4.1 How to Access

- Click \'My Courses\' in the left sidebar.

- Or click \'All Courses\' on the Dashboard\'s Continue Learning
  section.

## 4.2 Tabs

### My Courses Tab

Shows only the courses you are enrolled in. Each card displays:

- Course thumbnail image (or a book icon if none is set).

- Category badge (top-left of the thumbnail).

- MASTERY badge (top-right) if you have completed the course.

- A progress bar below the thumbnail showing completion percentage.

- Level badge (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced) and duration.

- Progress percentage.

- Course title and description.

- RESUME JOURNEY button -- click to open the course player.

- REVIEW NODE button -- shown when the course is completed.

### Explore Tab

Shows all available courses that you have NOT yet enrolled in. Each card
shows:

- Course details similar to My Courses.

- Price (or FREE label).

- ACQUIRE ACCESS button -- click to enrol (opens payment flow via
  Razorpay for paid courses).

## 4.3 Search & Filters

- Use the Search bar to filter courses by title or category.

- Use the Level filter buttons (All Levels, Beginner, Intermediate,
  Advanced) to narrow results.

## 4.4 Summary Stats (My Courses)

Three stat cards at the top show: total enrolled, completed, and
in-progress courses.

## 4.5 Split View / Interactive Lab Mode

- Click \'Split View Lab\' in the sidebar or the green promo card on the
  Dashboard.

- The courses page title changes to \'Interactive Lab\'.

- Click \'INITIALIZE LAB\' on any course to open it in Split View where
  the video player and a code editor appear side by side.

# 5. Attendance

The Attendance page lets you view your daily attendance records on a
monthly calendar.

## 5.1 How to Access

- Click \'Attendance\' in the left sidebar.

## 5.2 Stats at the Top

- Presence Velocity -- attendance percentage for the selected month.

- Audit Cycle -- total sessions recorded.

- System Exceptions -- number of absences.

## 5.3 Calendar View

- The calendar shows the current month by default.

- Each day cell is colour-coded: green tint for Present, red/pink tint
  for Absent.

- Today\'s date is highlighted with the primary colour.

- Future dates appear dimmed.

- Use the left/right arrow buttons to navigate to previous or next
  months.

## 5.4 Export Attendance

- Click the EXPORT JOURNEY button (top right) to download your
  attendance as an Excel file.

# 6. Assessments

The Assessments page lists all tasks and assignments assigned to you by
your mentors.

## 6.1 How to Access

- Click \'Assessments\' in the left sidebar.

## 6.2 Assessment Cards

Each card shows:

- Assessment title.

- Status badge: Pending (yellow), Submitted (purple), Reviewed (green),
  Overdue (red).

- Description / question.

- Due date, mentor name, and linked course name.

- Mentor feedback box (shown once the mentor has reviewed your
  submission).

## 6.3 Submitting an Assessment

- Step 1 -- Click Submit Answer on a pending assessment card.

- Step 2 -- A modal opens showing the task/question.

- Step 3 -- Type your answer in the Your Answer text area.

- Step 4 -- Click Submit Now.

> **📌 Note:** After submission the status changes to \'Submitted\'. You
> can still edit before the mentor reviews it by clicking \'View/Edit
> Submission\'.

# 8. My Documents

The Documents page lets you upload and manage your personal academic
documents securely.

## 8.1 How to Access

- Click \'My Documents\' in the left sidebar under LEARNING RESOURCES.

## 8.2 Document Cards

Each uploaded document shows:

- File icon.

- Document type name.

- Upload date.

- File extension badge (PDF, DOCX, etc.).

- Open icon -- opens the file in a new browser tab.

- Delete icon (trash) -- deletes the document after a confirmation
  prompt.

## 8.3 Uploading a New Document

- Step 1 -- Click the Upload New button (top right).

- Step 2 -- A modal appears. Select the Document Type from the dropdown:

Available types: Offer Letter, Internship Certificate, Experience
Letter, Marksheet / Scorecard, Other.

- Step 3 -- If you select \'Other\', enter a custom document name.

- Step 4 -- Click the file area or drag-and-drop to select a file from
  your device.

- Step 5 -- Click Save Document.

- Step 6 -- To cancel, click Cancel.

# 9. Certificates

The Certificates page displays all course completion certificates you
have earned.

## 9.1 How to Access

- Click \'Certificates\' in the left sidebar under LEARNING RESOURCES.

## 9.2 Certificate Cards

Each certificate card shows:

- Course name.

- Verified badge.

- Certificate ID.

- Issued date and course duration.

- Download Certificate PDF button.

## 9.3 Downloading a Certificate

- Step 1 -- Find the certificate card for the desired course.

- Step 2 -- Click Download Certificate PDF.

- Step 3 -- The PDF is generated and saved to your device automatically.

> **📌 Note:** Certificates can be independently verified at
> learn.simplesphere.in/certificates using the Certificate ID shown on
> the card.

## 9.4 How to Earn a Certificate

Complete all lectures, assignments, and quizzes in a course (scoring 75%
or more on quizzes). The certificate is issued automatically.

## 9.5 Refresh

- Click the Refresh button (top right) to reload and check for newly
  issued certificates.

# 11. AI Study Mentor

The AI Study Mentor is an AI-powered chat assistant that helps you
understand course concepts, answer questions, and guide your learning.

## 11.1 How to Access

- Click \'AI Study Mentor\' in the left sidebar under LEARNING
  RESOURCES.

- Or click the \'Chat Now\' button in the AI promo card on the
  Dashboard.

## 11.2 How to Use

- Step 1 -- Type your question or topic in the message input box at the
  bottom.

- Step 2 -- Press Enter or click the Send button.

- Step 3 -- The AI responds instantly with an explanation, example, or
  guidance.

- Step 4 -- Continue the conversation to ask follow-up questions.

# 15. Logout

Logging out ends your session and returns you to the Login page.

## 15.1 How to Logout

- Step 1 -- Scroll to the bottom of the left sidebar.

- Step 2 -- Click the red LOGOUT button.

- Step 3 -- A confirmation modal appears asking \'Are you sure you want
  to log out of SimpleSphere?\'

- Step 4 -- Click Logout to confirm, or Cancel to stay logged in.
