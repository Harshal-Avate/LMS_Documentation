# Logout

![Visual Guide](/images/students/logout.png)

Logging out ends your session and returns you to the Login page. Always log out to protect your account, especially on shared computers.

## How to Logout

**Step 1** " Scroll to the **bottom of the left sidebar**.

**Step 2** " Click the red **LOGOUT** button.

**Step 3** " A confirmation modal appears asking:
> _"Are you sure you want to log out of SimpleSphere?"_

**Step 4** " Click **Logout** to confirm and end your session.

**Step 5** " Click **Cancel** to close the dialog and stay logged in.

After logging out, you are automatically redirected to the **Login page**.

## What Happens When You Logout

- Your session cookie is cleared from the browser
- You are redirected to the `/login` page
- All unsaved work is discarded " save your work before logging out
- If "Stay logged in" was unchecked, you will need to re-enter your credentials next time

<Warning title="Always logout on shared computers">
If you are using a **shared, public, or lab computer**, always log out after your session. Failing to do so could allow the next user to access your account.
</Warning>

## Session Timeout

SimpleSphere may automatically log you out after a period of inactivity (the timeout period is set by your institution's administrator). If this happens:

1. You will be redirected to the login page with a session expired message
2. Log back in with your credentials
3. Any unsaved work may have been lost

<Info title="Persistent Login">
If you checked **"Stay logged in"** during login, your session will persist across browser restarts. You will remain logged in until you explicitly click **Logout** or your session expires due to a password change.
</Info>
