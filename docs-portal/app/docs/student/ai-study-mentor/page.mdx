# AI Study Mentor

![Visual Guide](/images/students/ai-study-mentor.png)

The AI Study Mentor is an AI-powered chat assistant that helps you understand course concepts, answer questions, and guide your learning journey.

## How to Access

- Click **'AI Study Mentor'** in the left sidebar under **LEARNING RESOURCES**.
- Or click the **'Chat Now'** button in the AI promo card on the Dashboard.

## How to Use

**Step 1** " Type your question or topic in the **message input box** at the bottom of the chat window.

**Step 2** " Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button.

**Step 3** " The AI responds instantly with an explanation, example code snippet, or detailed guidance.

**Step 4** " Continue the conversation to ask follow-up questions or dive deeper into a topic.

## What You Can Ask

The AI Study Mentor can help with:

| Topic | Examples |
|-------|---------|
| **Concept Explanations** | "What is recursion in Python?" |
| **Code Debugging** | "Why is my loop not working?" |
| **Study Guidance** | "How should I approach learning data structures?" |
| **Course Topics** | "Explain the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning" |
| **Project Ideas** | "Give me a project idea to practice JavaScript" |
| **Quick Revision** | "Summarize the key concepts in OOP" |

## Interface Overview

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Chat Window** | Displays the conversation history with you and the AI |
| **Message Bubbles** | Your messages on the right, AI responses on the left |
| **Input Box** | Where you type your questions |
| **Send Button** | Submits your message to the AI |
| **Suggestion Chips** | Quick-tap suggestion buttons for common queries |

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** " Instead of "explain Python", ask "explain list comprehension in Python with an example"
2. **Ask follow-ups** " The AI maintains conversation context so you can build on previous answers
3. **Request examples** " Ask for "an example with code" to see practical demonstrations
4. **Use it for revision** " Ask the AI to quiz you on a topic: "Quiz me on CSS Flexbox"

<Info title="AI Disclaimer">
The AI Study Mentor is powered by a large language model and provides responses based on your questions. Always verify critical information with your course materials or mentor, as AI responses can occasionally be inaccurate.
</Info>

<Note title="Conversation History">
Your chat history with the AI Study Mentor is preserved within your session. However, it may not persist across different login sessions. Save important explanations or code snippets locally.
</Note>
