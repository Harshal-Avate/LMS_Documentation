# Attendance

![Visual Guide](/images/students/attendance.png)

The Attendance page lets you view your daily attendance records on a monthly calendar.

## How to Access

- Click **'Attendance'** in the left sidebar.

## Stats at the Top

Three key metrics are displayed at the top of the page:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Presence Velocity** | Your attendance percentage for the selected month |
| **Audit Cycle** | Total sessions recorded in the system |
| **System Exceptions** | Number of absences logged |

## Calendar View

The attendance calendar gives you a visual month-by-month view of your presence record.

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Green tint** | Present on that day |
| **Red/Pink tint** | Absent on that day |
| **Highlighted date** | Today's date is highlighted in the primary color |
| **Dimmed dates** | Future dates appear greyed out |
| **Navigation arrows** | Left/right arrows to move between months |

## How to Read the Calendar

**Step 1** " Navigate to the **Attendance** page via the sidebar.

**Step 2** " The current month is displayed by default.

**Step 3** " Each day cell is color-coded:
  - 🟢 **Green** = Present
  - 🔴 **Red/Pink** = Absent
  - ⬜ **Blank/Grey** = No record / Future date

**Step 4** " Use the left and right arrow buttons to view previous or upcoming months.

## Export Attendance

You can download your attendance record as an Excel spreadsheet:

**Step 1** " Click the **EXPORT JOURNEY** button in the top-right corner of the page.

**Step 2** " Your attendance data is automatically downloaded as an Excel file (`.xlsx`).

**Step 3** " The file contains your name, dates, and attendance status for all recorded sessions.

<Info title="Attendance affects certificates">
You must maintain a minimum attendance percentage (typically 75% or as set by your institution) to be eligible for course completion certificates.
</Info>

<Note title="Cannot edit your own attendance">
Students can only **view** attendance records. If you believe your attendance is incorrectly recorded, contact your mentor or administrator to have it corrected.
</Note>
