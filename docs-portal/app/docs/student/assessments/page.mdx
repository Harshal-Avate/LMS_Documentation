# Assessments

![Visual Guide](/images/students/assessments.png)

The Assessments page lists all tasks and assignments assigned to you by your mentors.

## How to Access

- Click **'Assessments'** in the left sidebar.

## Assessment Card Overview

Each assessment card displays:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Name of the assignment |
| **Status Badge** | Current submission status (see below) |
| **Description** | The assignment question or task description |
| **Due Date** | Deadline for submission |
| **Mentor Name** | The instructor who assigned the task |
| **Course Name** | The course this assessment belongs to |
| **Feedback Box** | Shown once the mentor has reviewed your submission |

## Status Badges

| Status | Color | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Pending** | 🟡 Yellow | Not yet submitted |
| **Submitted** | 🟣 Purple | You have submitted but mentor hasn't reviewed |
| **Reviewed** | 🟢 Green | Mentor has reviewed and left feedback |
| **Overdue** | 🔴 Red | Past the due date without submission |

## Submitting an Assessment

**Step 1** " Click **Submit Answer** on a pending assessment card.

**Step 2** " A modal dialog opens displaying the full task/question.

**Step 3** " Type your answer in the **Your Answer** text area.

**Step 4** " Click **Submit Now** to submit your response.

<Note title="Editing a submission">
After submission, the status changes to **'Submitted'**. You can still edit your answer before the mentor reviews it by clicking **'View/Edit Submission'** on the card.
</Note>

## Viewing Mentor Feedback

Once a mentor has reviewed your submission:

1. The status badge changes from **Submitted** to **Reviewed** (green).
2. A **Feedback Box** appears at the bottom of the card with the mentor's comments.
3. You can read the feedback and use it to improve future submissions.

## Overdue Assessments

<Warning title="Overdue Assessments">
Assessments past their due date are highlighted with a **red Overdue badge**. While you may still be able to submit late, it could affect your progress score. Contact your mentor for clarification.
</Warning>

## Tips for Assessments

- Submit before the deadline to avoid the **Overdue** status
- Read the full question carefully before writing your answer
- After submission, check back to read your mentor's feedback
- Use feedback to improve on the next assessment
