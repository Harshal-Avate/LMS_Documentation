# Student Dashboard

![Visual Guide](/images/students/student-dashboard-overview.png)

The Dashboard is your home page. It gives a quick summary of your learning activity, upcoming sessions, and pending tasks.

## How to Access

- After login, you land here automatically.
- Click **'Overview'** in the left sidebar under **MY DASHBOARD**.

## Dashboard Sections

### Live Class Banner

If a mentor has started a live session, a purple animated banner appears at the top showing the session title and mentor name. Click **Join Now** to open the meeting link in a new tab.

### Survey Notification

If a feedback survey is pending for one of your courses, a banner appears prompting you to take it. Click **Start Survey**, answer the questions, then click **Submit Feedback**.

### Greeting & Activity Overview

A personalized greeting with your first name is displayed, along with an **'Activity Overview'** summary of your recent actions.

### Stat Cards

Four cards display your key metrics at a glance:

| Card | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Enrolled Courses** | Total number of courses you are enrolled in |
| **Attendance** | Your overall attendance percentage |
| **Pending Tasks** | Number of assessments not yet submitted |
| **Certificates** | Total certificates you have earned |

Clicking any stat card navigates to the related section.

### Continue Learning

Lists your enrolled courses. Click a course row to open it and resume learning from where you left off.

### Upcoming Sessions

Shows scheduled live sessions with time and date. Click the link icon to open the meeting in Jitsi.

### Pending Assessments

Shows assessments assigned to you that have not been submitted. Overdue items are highlighted in **red**. Click **View All** to go to the full Assessments page.

### Quick Actions

Buttons to quickly navigate to:
- **Split-View Workspace** " watch video and code simultaneously
- **AI Study Mentor** " AI chat assistant for study help
- **My Analytics** " detailed learning progress charts
- **Achievements** " view earned badges and certificates

### Interactive Lab Promo

A green card that opens the Split View workspace where you can watch a lecture and write code simultaneously in a side-by-side layout.

### AI Study Mentor Promo

A purple card that opens the AI chat assistant for instant study help on any topic.

<Info title="Live Class Alert">
When a mentor starts a live Jitsi class, a glowing banner appears at the very top of your dashboard. Don't miss it " click **Join Now** immediately to join the session.
</Info>
