# Preferences / Settings

![Visual Guide](/images/students/preferences-settings.png)

The Settings page lets you update your personal information, change your password, and configure account preferences.

## How to Access

- Click **'Preferences'** in the left sidebar under **SYSTEM**.

## Available Settings

### Personal Information

Update your profile details:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Full Name** | Update your display name |
| **Contact Number** | Update your phone number |
| **Email Address** | Your registered email (may require admin approval to change) |

### Password Change

Change your account password for security:

**Step 1** " Locate the **Security / Password** section in Settings.

**Step 2** " Enter your **Current Password** in the first field.

**Step 3** " Enter your **New Password** in the second field.

**Step 4** " Confirm the new password in the **Confirm Password** field.

**Step 5** " Click **Save Changes** to apply the new password.

<Warning title="Password Requirements">
Choose a **strong password** containing:
- At least 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (e.g., @, #, !)
</Warning>

### Theme Preferences

Toggle between light and dark mode:

- Use the **Theme Toggle** button in the sidebar or top header
- Your preference is saved to your browser session
- The platform defaults to your operating system's color scheme

## After Updating Settings

- Your profile changes are saved immediately
- Password changes take effect on your next login or immediately depending on the platform configuration
- Theme changes apply instantly across the entire platform

<Info title="Cannot change email yourself?">
If your email address needs to be updated, contact your assigned administrator. Email changes may require identity verification to prevent unauthorized account access.
</Info>

<Note title="Profile Photo">
SimpleSphere currently uses an auto-generated avatar based on your initials. Custom profile photo upload may be available in future updates.
</Note>
