# Announcements

![Visual Guide](/images/students/announcements.png)

The Announcements page displays important messages posted by admins or mentors for all students or specific batches.

## How to Access

- Click **'Announcements'** in the left sidebar under **MY DASHBOARD**.

## What You Will See

- A list of announcements with **title**, **date**, and **message body**.
- Announcements are shown in **reverse chronological order** (newest first).
- Pinned announcements appear at the top of the list, regardless of date.

## Announcement Card Details

Each announcement card displays:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | The subject of the announcement |
| **Message Body** | The full text content of the notice |
| **Priority Badge** | Color-coded: Urgent (red), High (orange), Medium (yellow), Low (grey) |
| **Audience Tag** | Whether it targets Everyone, Students Only, or Mentors Only |
| **Creator** | Name and role of the person who posted it |
| **Timestamp** | Date and time of posting |
| **Pin Indicator** | 📌 icon if the announcement is pinned at the top |

## Priority Levels

Announcements are categorized into four priority levels:

| Priority | Color | Use Case |
|----------|-------|----------|
| 🔴 **Urgent** | Red | Exam schedules, emergency notices |
| 🟠 **High** | Orange | Important deadlines, system updates |
| 🟡 **Medium** | Yellow | General reminders, schedule changes |
| ⚪ **Low** | Grey | Informational notices, tips |

<Info title="Pinned Announcements">
Critical announcements such as exam notices, holiday schedules, or major platform updates are **pinned** by admins and always appear at the top of the feed. Check pinned announcements first.
</Info>

## How to Read an Announcement

1. Navigate to **Announcements** in the sidebar.
2. Scroll through the list to find relevant notices.
3. Read the full message body on the card.
4. Note the priority badge and audience tag to understand urgency and relevance.

## Notification Alerts

New announcements may trigger **in-app notifications** visible in the notification bell icon in the top header. Click the bell to see recent alerts.

<Note title="Can students post announcements?">
No. Students can only **read** announcements. Only **Mentors** and **Admins** have the ability to post or edit announcements.
</Note>
