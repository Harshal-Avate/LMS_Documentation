# My Analytics

![Visual Guide](/images/students/my-analytics.png)

The Analytics page provides detailed insights into your learning progress and performance across all enrolled courses.

## How to Access

- Click **'My Analytics'** in the left sidebar.
- Or click the **'My Analytics'** button in the Dashboard Quick Actions.

## What Is Shown

### Progress Charts

Interactive charts for each enrolled course showing:
- **Lecture Completion** — percentage of lectures you have watched
- **Assignment Completion** — percentage of assignments submitted and reviewed
- **Quiz Scores** — your average quiz performance over time

### Assessment Scores & History

A tabular or graphical view of:
- All completed assessments and your scores
- Submission timestamps and review dates
- Mentor feedback summaries

### Attendance Trends

A line or bar chart showing your month-over-month attendance:
- Green bars for months with good attendance (>= 75%)
- Red bars for months with low attendance (&lt; 75%)

### AI-Generated Learning Insights

SimpleSphere's AI engine generates personalized insights including:

| Insight | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Learning Velocity** | Your pace compared to expected completion rate |
| **Projected Completion Date** | Estimated date to complete the course at current pace |
| **Focus Areas** | Topics where your quiz scores are below average |
| **Weak Topics** | Specific subjects requiring additional revision |
| **Strengths** | Areas where you consistently perform well |

## Understanding Your Progress

### Completion Rate

The completion rate is calculated as:

```
Completion % = (Completed Lectures + Completed Assignments) / Total Course Items Ã" 100
```

### Quiz Score Average

```
Avg Score = Sum of all quiz scores / Number of quizzes taken Ã" 100
```

### Learning Velocity

If your actual progress is below the expected progress for the current date, you will see a **lag indicator** showing how many days or weeks behind you are.

<Info title="AI Insights Disclaimer">
AI-generated insights are based on your activity data and are intended as **guidance only**. They do not replace personalized mentorship. If you have concerns about your progress, speak with your assigned mentor.
</Info>

## Taking Action on Insights

1. **If Learning Velocity is low** — Increase your daily study sessions or speak with your mentor for a revised study plan.
2. **If Focus Areas are highlighted** — Revisit the relevant course lectures and re-attempt any available quizzes.
3. **If Attendance is flagged** — Ensure you attend all scheduled live sessions and notify your mentor in advance of any absence.
