# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the SimpleSphere Learning Management System.

## General Questions

### What is SimpleSphere Learning?

SimpleSphere Learning is a comprehensive Learning Management System (LMS) designed for educational institutions, training centers, and bootcamps. It provides dedicated portals for Students, Mentors, and Administrators.

### Which web browsers are supported?

SimpleSphere works best with:
- **Google Chrome** (recommended)
- **Mozilla Firefox**
- **Microsoft Edge**
- **Safari** (macOS/iOS)

Internet Explorer is not supported.

### Is SimpleSphere available on mobile devices?

Yes. The SimpleSphere portal is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. For the best experience, use a modern mobile browser such as Chrome or Safari.

### How do I contact technical support?

Contact your institution's IT administrator or reach SimpleSphere support via the contact information provided during your enrollment.

## Student Questions

### How do I log in as a student?

Navigate to your institution's SimpleSphere URL and enter your assigned username and password. Click **Sign In**. You can also use **Sign in with Google** if your account is linked to Google.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page. Enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within 5 minutes.

### Why can't I see my certificate after completing a course?

Certificates are issued automatically when you complete all lectures, assignments, and quizzes (with a minimum score of 75%). Click the **Refresh** button on the Certificates page. If it still doesn't appear, contact your mentor to verify your completion status.

### How is my attendance percentage calculated?

Your attendance percentage is calculated as:

```
Attendance % = (Days Present / Total Recorded Days) Ã" 100
```

A minimum of **75% attendance** is typically required for certificate eligibility.

### Can I edit an assessment after submitting?

Yes. After submission, you can click **"View/Edit Submission"** on the assessment card to modify your answer **before your mentor reviews it**. Once reviewed, the submission is locked.

### What programming languages does the Code Arena support?

The Code Arena supports Python, JavaScript, Java, C, C++, and TypeScript. Additional languages may be added in future updates.

### How do I join a live class?

When your mentor starts a live Jitsi class, a purple animated banner appears at the top of your dashboard. Click **Join Now** to join the session in a new browser tab.

### Where can I verify my certificate?

Go to **learn.simplesphere.in/certificates** and enter your Certificate ID (visible on your certificate card) to verify authenticity.

## Mentor Questions

### How do I create a new course module?

Navigate to **My Courses → [Course Name] → Edit Syllabus** (Curriculum Builder). Click **Create New Module**, enter the title and description, and click **Save Module**.

### What is the SLA response threshold?

The SLA (Service Level Agreement) target for mentor responses is **48 hours**. If student doubts or assignment submissions go unreviewed beyond 48 hours, the SLA Monitor on your dashboard will display a Warning badge.

### How do I submit a course for admin review?

In the **Curriculum Builder**, click **"Submit to HQ"**. The course status changes to **Pending Approval** and the admin receives a notification to review it.

### Can I edit a course after submitting for review?

No. Once a course is in **Pending Approval** status, editing is disabled. Contact your admin to reject and return the course to Draft status if changes are needed.

### What happens if I miss the SLA window?

SLA breaches are logged in the system and reflected in:
- Your SLA Response Monitor on the dashboard
- The Quality Audit report in the Admin panel
- The Technical Session Logging breach indicator

Repeated SLA breaches may trigger a **Retraining Alert** visible to administrators.

### How do I schedule a Jitsi live class?

Click **Schedule** on your Mentor Dashboard. Fill in the Course, Title, Description, Scheduled Date/Time, and Duration. Click **Schedule Meeting**. The session appears in your Schedule Manager.

### How do I grade an assignment?

Navigate to **Assignment Submissions & Grading Ledger** → **Review tab**. Select the assignment, read the student's submission, type feedback in the Feedback Box, and click the **Send** icon. The status updates to "Reviewed".

## Admin Questions

### How do I add a new student?

Navigate to **Students → Add Student**. Fill in Name, Phone (10 digits), Email, Center, and Fee details. Click **Save**.

### How do I assign a mentor to a course?

Navigate to **Course Hub → [Course Name] → Edit**. In the Course Form Modal, select mentors from the **Assigned Mentors** dropdown. Click **Save**.

### How do I generate a completion certificate?

Navigate to **Certification** (under ACADEMICS). Fill in Student Name, Course Title, Duration, Certificate ID, Start Date, and End Date. Click **Generate & Download Certificate**.

### What is the difference between Super Admin and Center Admin?

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Super Admin** | Full access to all centers, users, and global settings |
| **Center Admin** | Restricted to data from their specific assigned center(s) |

### How do I export attendance records?

Navigate to **Attendance** (under CORE). Click the **Export** button. Choose your date range and center filter, then click **Export to Excel** to download the `.xlsx` file.

### What does "Sync Intelligence" do?

In the **Quality Audit** page, clicking **Sync Intelligence** triggers a backend recalculation of all quality metrics — CSAT scores, SLA compliance percentages, and at-risk entity counts — based on the latest data.

### How do I deactivate a user?

Navigate to **Students** or **Mentor Hub**. Search for the user. Click the **Edit** icon on their row. Toggle the status to **'Dropped'** or click **Deactivate**. The user's access is revoked immediately.

## Technical Questions

### The platform is showing an error banner on login. What should I do?

Check that:
1. Your username and password are correct (no extra spaces)
2. Your internet connection is stable
3. The platform server is reachable (try refreshing the page)

If the error persists, contact your IT administrator.

### My attendance calendar isn't loading. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R). If the issue persists, clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.

### Can I use SimpleSphere offline?

No. SimpleSphere requires an active internet connection. All data is stored on the server and accessed in real time.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. SimpleSphere uses:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Cookie-based session authentication
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Cloudinary for secure file storage

### How do I report a bug or technical issue?

Contact your institution's IT department or SimpleSphere technical support with:
- A description of the issue
- The URL/page where it occurred
- Your browser and operating system details
- Screenshots if possible
