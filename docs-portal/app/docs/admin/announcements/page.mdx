# Announcements

![Visual Guide](/images/admin/announcements.png)

The Announcements module is the communications platform for admins to create, pin, and target notices to the student and mentor communities.

## How to Access

- Select **'Announcements'** under the **CORE** group in the left sidebar.

## UI Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **New Announcement Button** | Opens the form to compose a notice |
| **Priority Filters** | Filter buttons: Urgent, High, Medium, Low |
| **Pinned Section** | Top-anchored feed of pinned announcements |
| **Announcement Cards** | Visual cards with title, message, priority badge, and audience tag |
| **Edit/Delete Actions** | Visible on cards the admin has authorization to manage |

## Creating an Announcement

**Step 1** " Click the **'New Announcement'** button at the top of the page.

**Step 2** " Enter the **Title** of the announcement.

**Step 3** " Compose the **Message** content in the text area.

**Step 4** " Set the **Priority level**:
  - 🔴 **Urgent** " critical alerts
  - 🟠 **High** " important notices
  - 🟡 **Medium** " general reminders
  - ⚪ **Low** " informational updates

**Step 5** " Select the **Audience**:
  - **Everyone** " shown to all users
  - **Students Only** " visible only in the student portal
  - **Mentors Only** " visible only in the mentor portal

**Step 6** " _(Optional)_ Check **'Pin this announcement'** to keep it at the top of the feed permanently.

**Step 7** " Click **'Post Announcement'** to publish immediately.

<Info title="Pinned Announcements">
Pinned announcements are critical for highlighting exams, holiday notices, or major system upgrades. They remain at the top of the feed regardless of their post date.
</Info>

## Filtering Announcements

Use the **Priority Filter** buttons to view announcements by urgency level:

| Filter | Shows |
|--------|-------|
| **All** | Every announcement regardless of priority |
| **Urgent** | Only urgent (red) announcements |
| **High** | Only high-priority (orange) announcements |
| **Medium** | Only medium-priority (yellow) announcements |
| **Low** | Only low-priority (grey) announcements |

## Editing an Announcement

**Step 1** " Locate the announcement card you want to edit.

**Step 2** " Click the **Edit (pencil)** icon on the card.

**Step 3** " Modify the title, message, priority, audience, or pin status.

**Step 4** " Click **'Update Announcement'** to save changes.

## Deleting an Announcement

**Step 1** " Locate the announcement card.

**Step 2** " Click the **Delete (trash)** icon.

**Step 3** " Confirm the deletion in the dialog.

<Warning title="Audience Targeting">
Ensure the correct audience is selected before posting. Posting a student-only notice to "Everyone" will also show it to all mentors.
</Warning>
