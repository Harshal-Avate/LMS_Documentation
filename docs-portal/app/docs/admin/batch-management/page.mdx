# Batch Management

![Visual Guide](/images/admin/batch-management.png)

The Batch Management panel allows administrators to select courses and analyze detailed student learning metrics for each enrolled batch.

## How to Access

- Select **'Course Students'** under the **ACADEMICS** group in the left sidebar.

## UI Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Course Selector Dropdown** | Filters student cards by their active course/batch |
| **Batch Analytics Overview** | Quick statistics showing enrollment counts |
| **Student Grid** | Cards for each enrolled student with progress bars |
| **Details Popup** | Modal with quiz ratings and enrollment date |
| **Batch Assignment Modals** | Popups to add students to courses |

## Student Progress Cards

Each student card in the grid displays:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Student Name** | Full name of the student |
| **Email** | Registered email address |
| **Lecture Progress Bar** | Purple bar showing % of lectures completed |
| **Assignment Progress Bar** | Green bar showing % of assignments submitted |
| **Enrollment Date** | When the student was added to this course |

## Analyzing a Batch

**Step 1** " Click the **Course Selector** dropdown at the top of the page.

**Step 2** " Choose the course you want to audit. The student grid populates instantly.

**Step 3** " Scan the progress bars to identify students who are lagging.

**Step 4** " Click on a student's card to open their **Analytics Modal**.

**Step 5** " Review their detailed statistics:
- Quiz score ratings
- Lecture completion count
- Assignment submission count
- Date of enrollment

**Step 6** " Click the **redirect button** in their detail card to open their full profile in the Students panel.

## Adding a Student to a Batch

**Step 1** " In the Batch Management view, click **'Add Student to Batch'**.

**Step 2** " A modal opens with a searchable student list.

**Step 3** " Select the student and confirm.

**Step 4** " The student's card appears in the batch grid.

<Note title="Identifying Lagging Students">
If a student's **Lecture Progress** bar is significantly lower than others in the same batch, click their card to view detailed analytics and use the redirect button to open their profile and contact them.
</Note>

<Info title="Real-time Data">
The batch analytics grid refreshes dynamically when you select a different course. All progress bars reflect the student's most recent activity.
</Info>
